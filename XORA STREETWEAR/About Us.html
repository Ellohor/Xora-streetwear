ABOUT OUR STORE

Xora is an online clothing store based for women. We have created a community of designers, artists and fans of anime from all over the globe. Our main goal is to popularize anime and expose Japanese animation's endless worlds to all!

 

We have improved the quality of our shirts to give you the softest product possible. We are now using high quality cotton and polyester to give you a long lasting as well as comfortable wear.

 

We are very happy to announce full returns and exchanges within 30 days of your purchase arriving. Shirts must be kept in the same condition they were sent in. Contact our team at hello@soraclothing.ca for this matter.
